Sweet Water is the Seattle band whose music combines elements of punk, pop, grunge and new wave to deliver on the promises of rock and roll.

Singer Adam Czeisler, guitarist Rich Credo, bassist Cole Peterson and drummer Chris Friel write songs, cut records, make videos and play shows to bring the rock to the people.

Albums include Superfriends, Suicide, Ter, Clear The Tarmac, Dance Floor Kills and Parc Boys.
